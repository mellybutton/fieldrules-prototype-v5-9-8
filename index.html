How BECAUSE works
The reasoning field that makes your rule library defensible — and gets smarter as you use it.

Every rule in FieldRules follows IF / THEN / BECAUSE. The IF and THEN describe what happens. The BECAUSE explains why — and that's the part only a human can write.

What makes a good BECAUSE:
→ Explains the consequence of not following the rule
→ References specific experience or data
→ Uses your own words, not corporate language
→ Gives a new team member the "why" behind the "what"
The BECAUSE field is never AI-generated
This is the one field in every rule that FieldRules will never auto-fill. AI can detect the pattern (IF/THEN), but only you know why it exists. That reasoning is what makes the rule library trustworthy — and what makes it impossible for a competitor to replicate.

FieldRules watches for 6 authoring habits that degrade reasoning quality. When it detects a pattern, it adapts the card you see to help you write a better BECAUSE.
